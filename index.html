Overcast

Free secure internet chat

Download for Windows

Here's what you get:

Secure

Since Overcast uses WebRTC, no chat data ever goes to the server. And with the app, you can even start your own server.

Free

Overcast is, and always will be free. It's also open souce, check it out on Github!

Account-less

You only need the app to create servers. Other people can go to your server's link, and chat from a web browser*.

Customisable

6 different themes to choose from. Custom themes coming soon!

Quick

Messages are delivered in less than a second!
